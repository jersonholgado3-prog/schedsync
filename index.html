Welcome to SchedSync

SchedSync is a web-based scheduling management system designed to streamline class, teacher, room, and event scheduling within the institution. It helps administrators create conflict-free timetables while allowing teachers and students to access real-time schedule updates anytime, anywhere.

Conflict Detection & Resolution
